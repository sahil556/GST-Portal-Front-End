Accrual Based Accounting System

The firm is specialized in providing services of designing, developing and implementing the accrual / Double Entry accounting system of accounting for Corporate and Non-Corporate entities as well as for Government and Semi- Government agencies.

The firm possesses the expertise to design Manual on Accounting for implementing the accrual-based accounting system in the organization & providing training to the employees of the organizations for the implementation of accrual / Double Entry accounting system.

The firm is engaged into various projects of conversion of Single-Entry Accounting System to Accrual / Double Entry System of Accounting Assignments carried out for various Government Sectors in different states of India.
