Analysis of the Financial Statement of client is very important for decision making. We use various tools which gives real time online analysis of business in the area of Sales, Purchases, Receivables, Payables, Cashflow and Comparison of various expenditures and Profitability. This helps the management for decision making.  We use tools which are on cloud base and can be easily use for the analysis of the clients financial data from anywhere -anytime.

Financial analysis does Test of Profitability by Debt Ratio, Current Ratio and Collection Period. Similarly it does Test of Financial health by Return on Sales, Inventory Turnover and Return on Investment.
